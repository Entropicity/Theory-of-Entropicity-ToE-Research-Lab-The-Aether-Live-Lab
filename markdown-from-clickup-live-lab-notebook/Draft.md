It posits that space, time, gravity, and quantum mechanics emerge from this underlying entropic flux.Key Philosophical and Mathematical PillarsThe Entropic Field: The universe is conceptualized as an entropic manifold where spatial and temporal gradients continuously drive physical reality.Obidi Action Principle: Similar to classical variational principles, this framework governs the evolution and optimization of entropic flow and complexity across the manifold.Master Entropic Equation (MEE): This is the core dynamical equation. Analogous to Einstein's field equations in general relativity, it treats the entropy field—rather than geometry—as the source of all forces.The Curvature Transfer Theorem: Instead of assuming a foundational spacetime stage, this theorem states that probability distributions (information) directly dictate physical spacetime curvature.Vis Viva Entropica (VVE): A core metaphysical concept proposing that the universe is not a static machine governed by dead laws, but a self-renewing, dynamic organism driven by transformative energy.Reinterpretation of Core PhysicsGravity as Entropic Pressure: Gravity is not a fundamental force, but a manifestation of entropic curvature and pressure as systems seek higher entropic equilibrium.The Speed of Light: Light is not an arbitrary cosmic constant. It is the maximum rate at which the entropic field can reconfigure itself.Time and Relativity: Time is the direct measure of entropic change.
