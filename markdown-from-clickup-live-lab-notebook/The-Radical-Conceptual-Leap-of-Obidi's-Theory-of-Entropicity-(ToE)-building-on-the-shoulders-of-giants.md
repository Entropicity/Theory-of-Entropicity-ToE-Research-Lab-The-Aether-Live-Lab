# The Radical Conceptual Leap of Obidi's Theory of Entropicity (ToE): Building on the Shoulders of Giants 

**John Onimisi Obidi’s Theory of Entropicity (ToE)** represents a radical conceptual leap that takes existing "emergent gravity" research to its absolute logical extreme. [1, 2, 3] 

While mainstream theoretical physicists use entropy as a tool to explain gravity, Obidi inverts the entire pyramid of physics, elevating entropy into the literal fabric of existence. [2, 4] 

## The Mainstream Research Context

To understand how far Obidi pushes this, it helps to look at the established "gravity-as-entropy" frameworks he is building upon and seeking to subsume: [1] 

* **Ted Jacobson (1995):*" Demonstrated that Einstein’s field equations can be derived as a thermodynamic equation of state. He showed that gravity behaves like thermodynamics, but still assumed a baseline spacetime geometry. [2, 5]
  
* **Erik Verlinde (2010):** Proposed the theory of entropic gravity, suggesting that gravity is not a fundamental force but an emergent macroscopic phenomenon (like elasticity) caused by changes in the information associated with the positions of material bodies. [2, 4]
  
* **Thanu Padmanabhan & Ginestra Bianconi:** Explored how the geometry of spacetime and gravitational structures can naturally emerge from information geometry and thermodynamic properties. [1, 2] 

## How Obidi Takes It to the Extreme

Mainstream physicists generally treat entropy as a secondary, statistical property—a way to count the hidden microstates of a system that already exists in space and time. [2, 6, 7] 
Obidi’s ToE completely flips this script. His framework moves from an "entropic description of gravity" to a full ontological entropic field: [2, 4] 

## Mainstream Emergent Gravity:

[Spacetime/Quantum States] ──> [Information/Entropy] ──> [Gravity Emerges]

Obidi's Theory of Entropicity (ToE):
[Universal Entropic Field] ──> [Information] ──> [Mass/Energy] ──> [Spacetime/Gravity Emerges]


* **From Property to Substance:**In ToE, entropy is no longer an adjective describing disorder; it is a fundamental, continuous physical field (an ontological substrate). Spacetime, matter, and fields are just localized condensations or ripples in this entropic ocean. [2, 4] 
* **The Ultimate Inversion:** Mainstream physics asks, "What is the entropy of a black hole's geometry?" Obidi asks, "How does the dynamics of the universal entropic field generate the illusion of geometry and gravity in the first place?" [2, 4] 
* **The Speed of Light Redefined:** While relativity treats the speed of light ($c$) as an axiomatic cosmic speed limit, ToE declares that the speed of light $c$ is merely the maximum rate at which this underlying entropic field can reconfigure itself and all interactions and phenomena. [4] 

By eliminating spacetime and quantum states as fundamental building blocks, Obidi's framework positions itself not just as an extension of Verlinde or Jacobson, but as a total re-foundation of physics based purely on entropic causation. [1, 8]
